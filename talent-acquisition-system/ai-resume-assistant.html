AI Resume Assistant
How to use this feature:

The AI Resume Assistant can answer questions about all uploaded resumes. It provides instant insights about candidates, their skills, and experience. You can also ask it to help shortlist candidates based on specific criteria.

Try asking:

  • "Show me the top candidates for software engineer"
  • "Who has experience with React and Node.js?"
  • "Which candidates have the highest match scores?"
  • "Tell me about [candidate name]'s skills"
  • "Add candidate 1 to shortlist"
